Color Palette

Sphere uses colors to help amplify the personality of our brand: crisp, balanced and recognizable. The aim of our palette is meant to enhance user experience and how they interact with our marketing and products.

Primary Colors

Primary colors are the core colors we use throughout our user interface. They should be used as the main colors to convey Sphere throughout our core products and marketing. Use 10% opacity stepper to create shades.

Gradient Color

A gradient is the gradual blending from one color to another. It makes objects stand out by adding a new dimension to the design and creating realism of the object. In simple terms, gradients add depth.
Use it for covers and backgrounds.

CSS Atributes
linear-gradient(345deg, #61B1E1 -1.5%, #13476A 100%);
