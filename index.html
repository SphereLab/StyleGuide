Sphere software Brand Guidelines & Presskit

This guide and resource library contains everything you need to design and write according to Sphere's guidelines. We created it to be a central source of truth that includes detailed answers on how to use some of our collateral. For instance, you can browse our logos, colors, typography, as well as our SMM materials. By striving to keep up a unified standard across our design flow, we ensure our brand and the experiences is consistent, accessible, and most importantly — enjoyable!

Not from around here? Nice to meet you!

The Sphere team loves to collaborate across departments — and not just internally. As we grow, it’s becoming more challenging to stay updated with the latest brand designs. Use this guide to keep up with our brand new ideas and stay on pace with Sphere.
