Typography

Sphere typography is organized to keep things clean, readable, and non-invasive.

Typeface

Sphere’s primary typeface is Lato font — designed with an upright stress, open forms, and a neutral, yet friendly, appearance. It was optimized for print, web, and mobile interfaces, and has excellent legibility characteristics.

For more info about color options with type, check out our Color Combinations page.
